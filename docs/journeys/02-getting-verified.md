# Stage 2 — Getting Verified
_You prove you're a real, eligible, serious person — so everyone you'll meet has done the same._

**Covers:** VER-1, VER-2, VER-4, VER-5, VER-7, VER-9, ELG-1, ELG-2

## The journey (plain language, numbered)
1. You start with a short checklist: **Identity**, **Education or Work**, **Marital status**, **Photo**. Everyone sees the same list — the requirements are identical whether you're a man, a woman, or non-binary.
2. **Identity first.** You scan your government ID (passport, Aadhaar via DigiLocker in India, driving licence, etc.) and take a quick "move your head" selfie so we know the ID is really yours and you're a live person.
3. **Prove your credential.** You pick the easiest path for you — verify a **work email**, connect **LinkedIn**, or **upload a document** (degree/offer letter). Source-checks (registrar, employer) beat plain uploads, so we nudge you toward the strongest one available.
4. **Marital status.** You attest single / divorced / widowed and affirm it's true. Divorced/widowed can add a document for a stronger badge; a bare attestation still works but shows as lower assurance.
5. (Optional) **Income band** — never required, always shown as a band, never a number. Skipping it costs you nothing.
6. **Your photo** gets checked against your verification selfie so it's genuinely you — not a stock, stolen, or AI face.
7. You watch a **live checklist** as each signal turns green. Some pass instantly; a few may go to a human reviewer with a time estimate.
8. When Identity + at least one eligibility route clears, you land on **"You're eligible"** with your badge set. You get in on **either** a top-alumni credential **or** a premium-professional achievement — no single institute list.
9. If something can't be verified, you see a **friendly, non-ranking explanation** plus how to add another signal or **appeal to a human**.

  - `False "duplicate" (identical twin / shared phone) → distinct gov IDs disambiguate; your account is allowed`
  - `Genuinely fraudulent → appeal upheld, evidence retained; decision always explained`
  - `Repeated frivolous appeals → rate-limited, but a first genuine appeal is always heard`
