_Raw agent output. Source of truth for the synthesized dossier._

## Summary

The verified-premium segment splits into two proven models: **Western "exclusivity gatekeeping"** apps (The League, Raya) that verify via LinkedIn/Instagram and gate on selectivity, monetizing through high subscriptions; and **South-Asian "serious-intent" matrimony** platforms (EliteMatrimony, Betterhalf, Aisle, Jodi365, Dil Mil) that verify via ID/selfie/manual vetting and monetize through concierge fees or success fees. Across both, **credential and identity verification is now the core trust product**, not a feature — NRI/diaspora families explicitly rank verified profiles and privacy as top expectations. Pricing spans an enormous range: EliteSingles/Dil Mil/Aisle at ~$20–40/month, Raya at $25–50/month, up to The League and elite concierge matrimony at $300–2,500/month or success-fee models. For an international, all-verified-premium-professional platform, the strategic whitespace is **broadening beyond a single alumni network or single ethnicity while keeping rigorous credential verification** — but this must be built on DPDP Act (India) and GDPR (EU/UK) compliant, consent-first data handling.

## Implications for iitiimrishte Positioning

- **Verification is the product, not a badge.** Broadening beyond IIT/IIM alumni shifts the moat from "which school" to "how rigorously we verify credentials, income, identity." Multi-signal verification (degree + employer/LinkedIn + government ID + selfie liveness) is the headline.
- **"Premium professional" is a proven, ownable middle ground** between a single alumni network and mass-market apps (EliteSingles 85% degree-holders; Jodi365).
- **Diaspora-first is validated and underserved at the premium tier** — genuine whitespace for verified, marriage-intent, international premium-professional spanning US/UK/UAE/Singapore diaspora + local professionals.
- **Price to a tiered international ladder**: self-serve premium ~$20–50/month + optional concierge/success-fee tier ($300+/month or one-time success fee). Regional pricing standard.
- **Curated scarcity drives demand** (Raya's 2.5M waitlist vs six-figure base).
- **Compliance designed in from day one** (DPDP unbundled/unconditional consent, GDPR); consent-first UX as a trust differentiator.
- **Learn from Betterhalf's wind-down**: manual verification + AI matching are capital-intensive; premium pricing is what makes rigorous verification sustainable.
