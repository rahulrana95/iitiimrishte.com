## Key findings
- **No platform-specific scam/breach/catfishing/honeytrap case** tied to iitiimshaadi.com found in indexed sources (mid-2026). Absence of evidence ≠ proven safety.
- **Core mechanism = manual document screening (degree/ID/marksheet), not identity verification.** Verifies credentials, not that the human is who/what they claim, marital status, or intent — the vectors fraud actually exploits. [PTC News](https://www.ptcnews.tv/iit-iim-shaadi-dot-com-bizarre-matrimonial-site-screening-degrees-id-cards-and-what-not)
- **Large unverified base:** ~30k of ~150k authenticated/active; fee reported ₹32,922 "until marriage." Majority of encountered profiles may be unverified/dormant. [PTC News](https://www.ptcnews.tv/iit-iim-shaadi-dot-com-bizarre-matrimonial-site-screening-degrees-id-cards-and-what-not)
- **No Aadhaar/KYC backend** — unlike Shaadi.com (added Aadhaar) and LoveVivah (UIDAI backend). Govt advisory pushed ID verification. Manual doc review is a weaker fraud control. [India TV govt advisory](https://www.indiatvnews.com/news/india-user-verification-with-valid-ids-must-on-matrimonial-sites-govt-advisory-332427) · [Forbes India Aadhaar](https://www.forbesindia.com/article/special/aadhaar-latest-tool-against-fake-matrimonial-profiles/50215/1)
- **Founder not IIT/IIM alum** — viral "dogalapan" trust hit for a verify-eliteness brand. [Zee News](https://zeenews.india.com/technology/ye-sab-dogalapan-hai-twitterati-trolls-iit-iim-shaadi-founder-for-not-passing-out-from-iit-or-iim-2449824.html)
- **Concentrated sensitive-PII collection** (degrees, IDs, marksheets) with no public data-safety disclosure, breach history, or security audit. [PTC News](https://www.ptcnews.tv/iit-iim-shaadi-dot-com-bizarre-matrimonial-site-screening-degrees-id-cards-and-what-not)
- **Gender-asymmetric verification** ("harder on men," "biased towards girls") — looser female-side onboarding is exactly the honeytrap/romance-scam seam. [Quartz](https://qz.com/india/2148968/elitist-iitiimshaadi-com-is-harder-on-men-than-women)
- Industry-wide matrimonial fraud (fake NRI/engineer grooms, honeytrap-extortion gangs) that credential-only screening does NOT stop. [Deccan Herald: six held](https://www.deccanherald.com/amp/story/india%2Fsix-held-for-duping-prospective-brides-through-fake-matrimonial-profiles-1218219.html)
- "Verified elite enclave" branding can breed false security (verified degree ≠ verified character/intent). [Homegrown](https://homegrown.co.in/homegrown-explore/iit-iim-shaadi-is-emblematic-of-the-systemic-elitism-in-indian-matrimony)

## Gaps / Pain points
- Verification verifies the wrong thing for fraud prevention (no liveness, no background, no KYC).
- Verified-vs-claimed member gap; no per-profile verified badge documented.
- Gender-asymmetric onboarding = fraud seam.
- Concentrated sensitive-doc collection, no public data-safety posture.
- No independent trust signal at scale (no Trustpilot/Sitejabber).
- Reputational trust deficit (non-alumnus founder + elitism backlash).
