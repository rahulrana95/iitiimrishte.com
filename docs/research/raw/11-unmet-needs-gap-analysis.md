# Research Angle: Unmet Needs / Churn / Gap Analysis (MOST IMPORTANT)

_Raw agent output. Target 403-blocked; from search snippets. Figures reported-not-verified._

## Prioritized gaps a better product must fill
1. **Thin, mostly-inactive inventory (#1 complaint).** DB "quite small," "many members on free accounts" → shortlists uncontactable. Only ~30k of ~150k active/authenticated. Need genuinely reachable active pool + honest active-user metrics. [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/in/app/iitiimshaadi-matchmaking-app/id1626456060) · [FAQs](https://iitiimshaadi.com/faqs)
2. **Structural gender imbalance leaving paying men with no responses.** ~60% M / 40% F; fewer women graduates → very low male response rates. Quartz: "harder on men than women." Paid man: "null and void" after 4 months. Must solve response-rate/liquidity for the majority side. [Quartz](https://qz.com/india/2148968/elitist-iitiimshaadi-com-is-harder-on-men-than-women) · [MatrimonialsIndia ratio](https://www.matrimonialsindia.com/profession/iitiim-matrimony.htm)
3. **Poor value-for-money.** "Inefficient and not worth the subscription fee"; ~₹32,922 "until marriage." Need outcome-linked / lower-commitment pricing + refund clarity. [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/in/app/iitiimshaadi-matchmaking-app/id1626456060) · [fee](https://iitiimshaadi.com/fee)
4. **No new-profile discovery — endless recycling.** "No convenient way to identify newly added profiles." ~500 new/week wasted without freshness signals/new-match alerts/dedupe of seen profiles. [chrome-stats reviews](https://chrome-stats.com/d/com.tycho.iitiimshadi/reviews)
5. **Slow, weak mobile app.** "Extremely slow"; heavy negative tail (~28% 1★ / ~14% 2★). Need fast reliable well-rated app. [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.tycho.iitiimshadi)
6. **The eligibility gate itself drives thin inventory + churn.** Strict 3–4-institute gate = "elitism within elitism," excludes compatible high-achievers. Could widen "elite" defensibly (achievement/verified-professional signals, not just a few institutes) to grow liquidity without losing curation. [Homegrown](https://homegrown.co.in/homegrown-explore/iit-iim-shaadi-is-emblematic-of-the-systemic-elitism-in-indian-matrimony)
7. **Trust deficit around "verified elite" promise.** Non-alumnus founder ("dogalapan"), KJo "segregation" backlash. Need authentic credible curation + transparent verification. [Onmanorama](https://www.onmanorama.com/news/india/2022/04/08/karan-johar-centre-of-debate-for-endorsing-matrimony-ad-iit-iim-shaadi.html)
8. **Concierge is opaque high-cost upsell with unproven ROI.** Premium buyers report null results → reads as upsell not outcome. Make human-assisted matchmaking outcome-transparent. [Quora](https://www.quora.com/How-effective-is-IITIIM-Shaadi-in-helping-me-get-the-right-partner-Is-the-hefty-subscription-justified)
9. **Low-signal search even for target users** — firsthand trials describe limited options. Search must surface enough relevant, contactable candidates. [ScoopWhoop](https://www.scoopwhoop.com/news/wedding-bells/)
10. **"Concept good, experience disappointing"** — classic churn signature (sign up, don't convert, quit). [Blind](https://www.teamblind.com/post/How-was-your-experience-with-iitiimshaadicom-o4d7fXwi)

**Top 3 (strongest, most repeated):** thin/inactive inventory · male-side response-rate collapse from gender imbalance · weak value-for-money.
