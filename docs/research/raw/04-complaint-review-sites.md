# Research Angle: Consumer Complaint & Review Aggregators

_Raw agent output. Aggregator hosts 403-blocked; from search snapshots._

## Key findings
- Complaint-board footprint is **thin** — no dedicated MouthShut/Trustpilot/Sitejabber/Scamadviser/BBB/PissedConsumer/ConsumerComplaints page for iitiimshaadi.com was found. Negativity clusters in app-store ratings + social/press controversy. **Guard against conflation:** matrimonial-fraud FIR/police items under this query actually belong to Shaadi.com, not iitiimshaadi.
- **Google Play ratings bimodal:** 29 reviews — 52% 5★, 7% 4★, 0% 3★, 14% 2★, 28% 1★ (~42% are 1–2★). [chrome-stats reviews](https://chrome-stats.com/d/com.tycho.iitiimshadi/reviews)
- **#1 paid-user complaint = value-for-money, not fraud:** "inefficient and not worth the subscription fee," small database, many free accounts (shortlists don't respond), slow app, no way to surface new profiles. [chrome-stats](https://chrome-stats.com/d/com.tycho.iitiimshadi/reviews)
- Six-month membership reported ~₹26,000; "hefty subscription justified?" openly debated. [Quora](https://www.quora.com/How-effective-is-IITIIM-Shaadi-in-helping-me-get-the-right-partner-Is-the-hefty-subscription-justified) · [fee](https://iitiimshaadi.com/fee)
- **JustDial:** ~4/5 across ~147 ratings; praise for verified/educated profiles + helpful staff; recurring negative = "lack of response from other users." [Justdial](https://www.justdial.com/Delhi/Iitiimshaadi-Dlf-City-Phase-4/011PXX11-XX11-220521175402-C1X3_BZDET)
- Elitism backlash = biggest reputational liability (Quartz "harder on men"). [Quartz](https://qz.com/india/2148968/elitist-iitiimshaadi-com-is-harder-on-men-than-women)
- Founder-"hypocrite" credential attack. [Storypick](https://storypick.com/iitiimshaadi-com-ceo/)
- Karan Johar promo amplified criticism. [Logical Indian FB](https://www.facebook.com/logical.indian/posts/4872174769578870/)
- Positive differentiator: mandatory doc verification + manual photo approval (anti-fake-profile). [loving.is](https://www.loving.is/en/review/iitiimshaadi-com/)

## Gaps / Pain points
- Value-for-money is #1 pain (₹26k/6mo vs small, unresponsive base).
- Liquidity/network-effect problem — niche gating throttles usable pool.
- App slow; no new-profile discovery.
- Reputational risk in press/social, not complaint boards.
- Thin independent-review signal (few app-store entries + Q&A).
- Refund policy opaque; no money-back terms surfaced.
- Fake-profile mitigation claimed but not externally verified.
