## Key findings
- App exists on both stores: Android `com.tycho.iitiimshadi`, iOS `id1626456060`, published by ALMA MATER MATTERS PRIVATE LIMITED (Social Networking category, iOS v1.4 ~33MB, released Aug 2023, updated May 2024). [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.tycho.iitiimshadi&hl=en_US) · [AppAdvice](https://appadvice.com/app/iitiimshaadi-matchmaking-app/1626456060)
- **Low volume:** ~20,000 Android installs; AppBrain shows 0 aggregated Play ratings; iOS ~1 rating → no meaningful star average. [AppBrain](https://www.appbrain.com/dev/IITIIMShaadi/)
- Older package `com.iitiimshadi.almamater` removed from Play 9 Nov 2023 → reviews didn't carry over (explains thin base). [APKPure](https://apkpure.com/iitiimshaadi-exclusively-for-the-highly-educated/com.iitiimshadi.almamater)
- **Positive theme — verification/safety:** "extremely safe and secured platform with genuine profile," "won't find any fake profiles," "good profile verification and genuine matches," "exceptional matches." [App Store reviews](https://apps.apple.com/in/app/iitiimshaadi-matchmaking-app/id1626456060?see-all=reviews&platform=iphone)
- **Negative — performance/bugs:** "extremely slow," "screens stuck on loading."
- **Negative — small DB / free accounts:** "database quite small," "many members on free accounts," "no convenient way to identify newly added profiles."
- **Negative — price/value + support:** "very expensive," "not worth the subscription fee," customer service "painfully slow," "days to get a response."
- Independent verdict (Medium, year-long user): ranks IITIIMShaadi #3 among Indian matrimony apps; "make an account if your college is in their list, but don't buy the subscription unless they offer lifetime in 10k." [Medium](https://medium.com/@abhishekb913/best-matrimonial-apps-in-india-eef8c5fe5055)

## Gaps / Pain points
- App performance (slowness) = top technical grievance.
- Liquidity / small active pool + poor new-profile discovery.
- High subscription judged "not worth it."
- Slow customer support.
- Notably NO fake-profile/auto-debit/refund complaints (unlike Shaadi.com) — verification is a real differentiator.
