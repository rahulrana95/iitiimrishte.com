## Beachhead recommendation
**Bengaluru senior tech/startup professionals × the UK-Indian (London) diaspora of the same cohort — one bilateral corridor, launched as a single market.** Not two markets — one corridor. A Bengaluru product leader and a London-based Indian-origin consultant are, for matchmaking purposes, the same pool (age 28–38, same professional altitude, overlapping culture) and already want to match with each other.

### Why fastest to liquidity
- Highest verifiable supply density for the eligibility gate (FAANG/unicorn/startup emails + LinkedIn → minutes to verify, no manual marksheet queue → low cost per verified user; attacks Betterhalf's capital-intensity failure).
- Bilateral corridor → both sides seed each other on a *desired* axis.
- Defuses elitism backlash (achievement-based, not 3-institute list) AND gender-collapse risk (control ratio by invite).
- English-first, mobile-first, high-trust, high-ARPU ($30–50/mo tolerated).
- Dense word-of-mouth graph (shared Slack/WhatsApp/alumni) → near-zero CAC.
- Rejected: "all India metros" (thin everywhere), "US diaspora first" (dispersed across NY/SF/Seattle), "non-Indian global premium" (no cultural pull to seed — save for V2).

## Scarcity/waitlist mechanics
- "Apply for an invitation," never "Sign up" — verification before queue.
- Queue position visible; move up by referring qualified peers + completing verification.
- Admit weekly cohorts of ~150–250, hand-balanced for ratio; if men over-apply, admit women faster and let male queue lengthen (lengthening queue *increases* perceived scarcity).
- Honest scarcity: "we open a city only when it's full enough to be worth your time."
- Status artifacts: "Founding Member — Bengaluru·London Cohort 01" badge, gift invite codes.

## Top 5 launch risks
1. Cold-start empty room → corridor design + hand-seed 150 balanced anchors + waitlist pacing + hard G1 gate.
2. Gender-response collapse → ratio by invite; reply-nudges + inbound caps; reply-parity is a launch gate (G2); no paid until gap ≤12pts.
3. Verification friction/cost → wedge chosen for automatable verification; premium/concierge funds manual review; never trade rigor for volume.
4. Scarcity misfires → tie to genuine density readiness, communicate honestly, queue-jump-by-referral keeps funnel growing, grandfather founders.
5. Trust/safety/privacy incident → non-enumerable IDs, isolated vault, encryption, moderation SLAs before Cohort 01; pen-test + moderation dry-run wk −1; publish data-safety posture.
