# Team Role: Male Acquisition & Monetization

_Raw agent output (condensed)._

**Governing rule:** male intake is a valve, not a firehose — pace male supply to female reachable capacity; monetize *reachability*, not outreach volume. Incumbent sells contact-unlock volume (20/50/150/500) = more of the thing that doesn't work; we sell the scarce thing (a reply/curated intro) and cap the unscarce thing.

## Acquisition channels (all throttleable)
Waitlist-gating (master valve) · alumni/employer referrals (expiring, capped) · professional-community partnerships · LinkedIn precision paid (budget dial) · content/PR counter-narrative · concierge outbound (fill specific gaps). **Ratio governor:** if projected 14-day male reachability would fall below target, male intake throttles automatically; marketing can't override.

## Keeping men engaged when replies are scarce
- Quality-over-quantity: daily curated 5–7 reachability-scored picks; scarce outbound budget; effort-gated first messages.
- **Radical reachability transparency** (answers "null and void"): reachability labels per profile ("Active this week," "typically replies," "high inbound ~15% reply"); personal reachability dashboard; honest expectation-setting at signup (the wound is broken-promise, not just low numbers).
- Coaching: profile-strength score + concrete fixes; a "win that isn't a reply" (views, shortlists, badges).
- Curation as pressure-release (concierge pre-warmed intros).

## Monetization — tiers
- **Tier 0 Verified Free:** verify, browse real pool, reachability labels, 3 interests/week.
- **Tier 1 Connect (self-serve):** full messaging, curated picks, who-viewed-me, dashboard, coaching, incognito. ~$20–50/mo band.
- **Tier 2 Priority:** larger curated allowance, priority ranking, monthly review, reachability guarantee.
- **Tier 3 Concierge:** human RM, hand-curated intros, milestone-based, published scope.

**Connect:** US $34/mo, $228/yr ($19/mo) · India ₹1,499/mo, ₹9,999/yr (~66% under incumbent ₹29,500, hits "10k" anchor) · UK £29/mo, £189/yr · UAE AED129/mo · SGD45/mo. Quarterly = featured default (fixes incumbent's tiny 6mo↔annual gap).
**Priority:** US $69/mo, $468/yr · India ₹2,999/mo, ₹19,999/yr · UK £59/mo · etc.
**Concierge:** US $1,200 (3mo)/$2,000 (6mo) + optional $1,500 success bonus · India ₹45,000/₹75,000 + ₹50,000 · published scope/milestones.

### Outcome-linked pricing (the value answer)
- Reachability SLA: commit to ≥8 verified reachable matches/mo; miss → paid time auto-extends free (visible counter).
- First-reply floor: zero genuine replies to quality outreach in a cycle (and we under-served) → cycle credited (guardrailed by min profile-strength + effort).
- Concierge milestones refundable per published schedule.
- Converts our biggest risk into a bounded liability controlled by throttling intake + a claim no incumbent can match.

### Refund/renewal transparency
Full pricing+renewal date+amount+refund terms on one screen before charge; no forced auto-renew; pro-rated cooling-off (7–14d); one-tap cancel; trackable refund status.

## Defusing "reverse dowry"/status anxiety
De-emphasize income/status as ranking currency (compatibility weights values/intent, income optional badge never sort key); kill public "thousands of interests" signal (inbound caps); symmetric verification; reframe status→seriousness ("responsive & serious" badge); private coached dignity-preserving feedback; no caste/institute hierarchy.

## Retention/LTV levers
Reachability SLA · momentum signals · quarterly-default plans · coaching · pause>cancel · transparent concierge upsell (triggered by genuine low-reachability signal) · graceful "found someone" offboarding→referral · win-back on verified re-entry.

## Male activation funnel + churn metric
Activation = **first genuine reply**, not first payment. **Primary churn predictor: Time-to-First-Reply (T14 reachability)** — % of new men getting ≥1 genuine reply to quality outreach within 14 days. Falling T14 is the earliest churn alarm; pair with reply-parity; feeds the ratio governor + outcome pricing + transparency. One metric governs acquisition pacing, monetization, retention.
