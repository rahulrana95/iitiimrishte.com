# Team Role: Partnerships & Community

_Raw agent output (condensed)._

**Core insight:** incumbent's problem is structural cold-start, not marketing. Partnerships solve all three hardest problems at once — cold-start liquidity (pre-qualified batches), gender balance (alumnae/women's networks let us acquire the scarce side first), and trust (peer vouching). **Partnerships ARE the moat** — a verification stack is copyable in 18mo; exclusive supply agreements + a vouch graph compound and can't be bought.

## Supply-side partnerships
Score partners on Density, **Balance (highest)**, **Trust-transfer (highest)**, Verification-assist.
- **Tier 1 (launch, 0–6mo, before any ad spend):** (1) **Alumnae & women's professional networks FIRST** — IIM/IIT women's chapters, Lean In India, Sheroes, WICCI, FICCI FLO, Ellevate, Women in Tech, B-school women's clubs; over-invest, let it run ahead of male side. (2) Alumni associations of 2–3 anchor institutes in ONE metro (PanIIT, IIM-A/B/C, ISB, BITSAA), geo-focused.
- **Tier 2 (6–12mo):** employer/HR perks (5–8 flagship employers, confidential, supplies employment attestation); professional bodies (IMA, bar associations, TiE/Headstart/founder communities).
- **Tier 3 (12–24mo):** diaspora orgs.

### Deal structures
- **A. Member-benefit/affinity (associations):** endorse as preferred benefit; members get waived fee + fast-track verification + founding discount; association gets NO cash (community fund / success-donation to scholarship instead — avoids "selling members" optics); **exclusivity clause (12–24mo) = where the moat is built**.
- **B. Employer perk:** benefits marketplace, confidential, employer provides employment attestation.
- **C. Community-sponsorship (TiE/WICCI):** sponsor events for warm member access.
- **D. Revenue-share/affiliate:** commercial partners & influencers only — never trusted associations (cash-per-head cheapens endorsement).
Playbook: warm intros via founding advisors → lead with member benefit → risk-free 1-chapter pilot → solve their engagement problem (bring events) → exclusivity in writing after pilot.

## Community / events (the liquidity engine)
Events break the chicken-egg because **you control exactly who's in the room** — a curated gender-balanced verified guest list IS a liquidity guarantee.
- Portfolio: curated dinners (8–12, 2–4/mo, balanced by construction) · mixers (40–80/mo, gender-capped tickets) · themed salons/expert dinners (draw scarce senior-female side with intellectual value, not "singles event") · webinars (weekly, diaspora nurture) · city chapters.
- **Gender balance in person:** women's tickets free/discounted, men's fund the event; never over-sell abundant side; brand as professional/experiential not "matrimonial" (Trojan horse for balance); bring-a-peer for scarce side.
- Launch ONE city deeply (Bangalore or Delhi-NCR) before spreading; chapter "alive" = self-sustains ~1 event/mo. Members-only verified Discord/Slack/WhatsApp keeps community warm, reduces churn.

## Referral & ambassador
- **Vouch mechanic** (core innovation): verified member vouches for a peer → visible "verified & vouched-for by [Name]" trust signal; rate-limited + reputation-weighted (bad vouches cost standing). Implements PRD "verified ≠ vouched."
- Referral: give-get, reward on verified activation, **balance-weighted (larger reward for scarce side)**, cohort bonuses.
- Ambassadors (deep, high-trust nodes): chapter leaders/senior professionals host events + originate association deals; get free premium + status + event budget + (top) rev-share/equity-lite; tiered (Founding → City Lead → Chapter Host); vet like members.

## Verification partners (buy, don't build; cut unit cost + raise trust)
- Identity+liveness India: **Aadhaar eKYC/DigiLocker** via Signzy/IDfy/HyperVerge/Bureau (closes incumbent's Aadhaar gap).
- Identity global: Onfido/Persona/Veriff/Sumsub.
- Credential: NAD/DigiLocker, institution-email, registrar APIs, **alumni-roll cross-check** (partnership doubles as verification).
- Employment: LinkedIn verification, work-email, Truework/employer-HR attestation, professional registries.
- Income: account-aggregator (India)/salary-slip/ITR, Plaid-equivalents.
- Marital status: attestation + escalating checks. Photo: liveness-linked + reverse-image.
- **First integration: DigiLocker + Aadhaar eKYC + alumni-roll cross-check** — beats spoofable-marksheet, cheap because partnership supplies roster; "government-grade verification" narrative asset.

## International/diaspora
Hub sequencing (seed off local partner cohort, one at a time): UK London → UAE Dubai → Singapore → US (Bay/NYC). Partner types: diaspora alumni chapters (PanIIT UK/USA, IIM/ISB/BITS global), diaspora professional/cultural bodies (NISAU, City Sikhs, SICCI, TiE global, NetIP), local non-Indian premium networks (Ivy/Oxbridge/INSEAD clubs) for the non-diaspora persona. **Killer feature: cross-hub + back-to-India matching** ("London↔Bangalore") no single-market rival spans. Data-residency + dual GDPR/DPDP precede each hub.

## The moat (why uncopyable)
(1) Exclusivity locks up supply channels. (2) Trust doesn't transfer twice — an association can't re-endorse a rival without burning credibility (winner-take-all per network). (3) The vouch graph compounds (network effect on *trust*). (4) Community is embodied, not digital (can't clone the London Tuesday dinner). Flywheel: partner cohort → balanced curated event → in-person trust+activation → members vouch → vouch graph deepens → better outcomes → more associations partner (exclusively) → repeat, raising the wall.

## Traps to avoid
Acquire scarce side first always · never pay associations per head · don't spread cities thin · reward verified activation not signups · keep employer perks confidential/opt-in · brand events "salon" not "singles night" · vet ambassadors like members · sign exclusivity only after successful pilot.
