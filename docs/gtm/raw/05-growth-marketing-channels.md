# Team Role: Growth Marketing & Channels

_Raw agent output (condensed)._

**Governing idea:** acquisition strategy IS inventory strategy. (1) Ratio not volume is the north star — a channel bringing 10k men + 400 women is negative-value. (2) Density before breadth — 5k in Bangalore+London-diaspora beats 50k scattered. Trust + liquidity are the growth engine, not budget.

## Channels ranked (CAC = blended per *verified* member)
1. **Referral & vouching loops** ★★★★★ $8–25 — core engine, invitees inherit inviter quality.
2. **Curated community / founder-led vetting** ★★★★★ $15–40 — cold-start + scarce-side supply.
3. **Waitlist + scarcity** ★★★★★ $5–20 — ratio valve at the door.
4. **Content & SEO (anti-fake narrative)** ★★★★ $10–30 compounding — own the critical-SERP whitespace.
5. **Success-story amplification / PR** ★★★★ $12–35 — real couples, not celebrity.
6. **Influencer (micro, niche, 70/30 female-weighted)** ★★★ $30–80 — credibility lenders, no mega/celebrity.
7. **Campus/corporate/alumni partnerships** ★★★ $20–60 — institutional trust + verification shortcut.
8. **Paid social (gender-gated fill + retargeting only)** ★★ $40–120 — pause if male CAC < female CAC.
9. **Paid search (high-intent harvest)** ★★★ $25–70 — "iitiimshaadi alternative/refund/not working," competitor brand terms.

## Referral/viral loops (mechanism-level)
- **Loop A "Invite a Peer":** triggered post-verification, post-first-match, at renewal; invitee skips waitlist at "vouched-in" tier; reward vests on *verification* not signup; **ratio multiplier 2–3× for referring scarce gender**; cap ~5/quarter. k ≈ 0.3×2×0.4 = 0.24 (cuts CAC ~20–25%).
- **Loop B "Vouch for a Friend"** (the differentiator no incumbent has): create a nominated profile for a friend, carrying "Vouched by verified member" badge; acquires people not actively looking; voucher's reputation on the line (self-policing); "vouch score" status game; family-vouch extension (arranged-adjacent, authentic).
- **Loop C Success-Story Amplification:** consented couple co-authors story + shareable card + bonus; feeds SEO/PR/social-proof/honest-outcomes counter; ~1 story per 50–100 marriages.
Anti-abuse: rewards vest on verified activation, identity-linked.

## Content & SEO ("anti-fake" moat)
Rank for: competitor/alternative terms (fast wins) · anti-fake/safety cluster (narrative moat) · premium-professional & diaspora intent · honest-metrics thought-leadership. Pillars: "we publish real active-user numbers," "verified ≠ vouched," "fairness is a feature," credible founder voice. Programmatic SEO `/verified-matchmaking/[profession]/[city]`, `/[country]-nri-matrimony`. Cadence 2 pillar + 4 long-tail + 1 success story/mo, metro-localized.

## Waitlist/scarcity
Public waitlist day zero; position + earned skips (refer/vouched/verify); **asymmetric gating** (women immediate+free, men queued — the key move); metro-batched invites at ~500–1,000 balanced verified locally. Illustrative: 10k waitlist → 4k qualified → 2k invited balanced → ~1,200 verified active at ~52/48.

## 12-month funnel model (illustrative, 1 metro + 1 diaspora hub)
Waitlist 55,000 → Registered 45% (24,750) → **Verified 60% (14,850)** → Active 70% of verified (~10,400) → Paid 8% of verified (~1,190). Contrast: incumbent 500k claimed / 30k active (6%); us 14,850 verified / 10,400 active (70%).
Ratio held ~52/48; reply parity within 10pts (throttle male acq if it breaks).
**Economics:** Premium ARPU ~$35/mo, ~7mo tenure → ~$245/paying member; blended LTV/verified ~$45–70; contribution LTV/verified ~$32–50; target CAC ≤$20–25/verified → CAC:LTV ~1:2–2.5 per verified, ~1:8–10 per paying; payback ~4–6 months cohort-level.
Spend mix: 40% referral+community, 25% content/SEO, 10% PR, 10% micro-influencer, 15% paid (gated).

## What NOT to do
No broad ungated paid social · no celebrity ambassador · no "tier-1 only"/institute-supremacist framing · no inflated vanity "members" number · don't incentivize raw signups · don't open a metro before dense+balanced · no mega-influencers/growth-hack stunts · no fake queues/dark-pattern scarcity · no bought contact lists/WhatsApp blasts · don't let acquisition outrun trust & safety.

**Flywheel:** honest content → gender-gated waitlist → community/vouching seed balanced dense pool → real matches/marriages → success stories (PR/SEO/proof) → ratio-weighted referrals → repeat, clone metro by metro. Paid only fills gaps.
