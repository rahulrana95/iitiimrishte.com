# Team Role: Female-First Growth & Trust/Safety (the constrained side)

_Raw agent output (condensed)._

**Core:** women are the constrained side; the economy runs on their trust. Incumbent's "thousands of interests/week" is a woman *drowning* — an untriageable, untrustworthy inbox. Men's "no replies" and women's "unusable flood" are the *same defect* from two sides. Optimize for **female-side experienced quality** (reply-worthiness of her inbox + her control over it). "Build the women's side first. The rest follows."

## Why premium women avoid these platforms (each tied to a documented failure)
Treated as scarce inventory (contact-unlock monetization) · inbox flood with no filter · asymmetric verification cuts against her trust of men · "verified elite" = false safety (degree ≠ character) · privacy exposure (enumerable IDs, no incognito/photo control) · elitism/"trophy" branding repels · family co-decides but has no safe seat.

## Trust architecture (5 commitments)
1. Symmetric verification, **visibly** (show his badge provenance in her UI).
2. "Verified ≠ vouched," said out loud.
3. She controls the flood, structurally (quality-gating + inbound caps).
4. Her data is a vault, not a directory (non-enumerable IDs, incognito, photo control, published DPDP/GDPR).
5. A real human on her side (staffed female-informed T&S escalation, SLA).

## Female acquisition (trusted intermediaries, not paid interruption)
- **Seed women FIRST:** women-only founding cohort recruited by hand (500–1,000 verified women/city before opening male registration beyond waitlist). Scarcity pointed at the constrained side; male scarcity becomes the marketing tension.
- Women's professional/alumnae networks (core channel): IIM/IIT women's chapters, Lean In India, SheThePeople, Women in Tech, Ellevate, Chief, FICCI FLO/CII IWN, women in finance/medicine/law; diaspora women's collectives. Format = a *verified private matchmaking circle* for that network, named liaison, priority verification.
- ~15–25 **real women ambassadors**/market (founder, physician, lawyer, professor, operator) hosting curated dinners — inverse of the KJo celebrity mistake; no "trophy" language.
- Asymmetric referral: a woman referring a verified woman earns materially more; private revocable invite she controls.
- Women-led editorial (safety, red-flag literacy, scam awareness); private moderated women's community; radical transparency (publish honest active-female metrics + reply parity).
- Consented family-collaborator flow ("involve family on your terms").

## Women-first product features
- **Control the flood (#1 retention lever):** scarce+costly outreach for senders/free for her; inbound caps she controls; reply-worthiness ranking of inbox; **no penalty for not replying** (apply PRD's reply-nudges/expiry to SENDERS not oversubscribed women — explicit PRD correction).
- **Women-initiate lane** (Bumble-style, marriage-intent): only she opens; mutual-interest-gated contact as default; "curated for you" not "browse the catalog."
- **Privacy/incognito/photo control:** incognito by default option (first-run); photo ladder (blur-until-mutual, per-photo, watermark/screenshot-deter, face-optional since verification carries trust); non-enumerable IDs; per-field visibility (employer masked to "verified at [Big Tech/Top Consulting/Hospital]" until reveal); scrape/screenshot defenses + anomalous-access alerts.
- **Verification symmetry surfaced:** show which of 5 signals he cleared; **mandatory prominent marital-status attestation for men** (the honeytrap vector women fear most).
- **Anti-harassment/block/report/escalation:** inline one-tap; auto-filter unsolicited explicit/abusive/contact-fishing; **identity-linked bans survive re-registration** (verification moat as safety weapon); **silent block**; report actioned within SLA + status told back; safety-escalation lane for threats/extortion.
- Consented scoped family collaborator.

## Safety — two divergent threat models (PRD treats as one)
- **A: she is fraud target** (romance/financial scam) → multi-signal verification + marital-status attestation + duplicate/stock-photo detection + in-context scam nudges + "verified≠vouched."
- **B: she is harassment/exposure target** → silent block, identity-linked bans, anti-harassment filtering, escalation lane, photo/employer masking, scrape defense.
- Cross-cutting: isolated PII vault, staffed female-informed T&S, Women's Safety Advisory tunes fraud models.

## Brand tone
Say: "For people who've built a serious life — and want a partner who has too"; "Verified. Private. On your terms."; "You decide who reaches you." Warm, adult, peer-to-peer. Never: "eligible bachelors/trophy/catch/marry an IIT boy," pedigree/caste hierarchy, celebrity trust-anchor. Litmus: would an accomplished 30-yo woman feel *respected as the decider* or *sorted as inventory*?

## Female activation funnel + top 3 retention levers
Activation = "had one safe, high-quality, self-controlled interaction" (not "signed up"). First-run privacy setup before she's visible; curated shortlist Day 1.
Retention: (1) **inbox quality & control** (the single most important number in the company); (2) felt safety & control that compounds; (3) visible momentum + Women's Advisory Council (respected as stakeholder, not farmed).

## Female-side scorecard
Female inbox-quality index · reply-rate parity + female inbound within manageable band · incognito/photo-control adoption · report→resolution time + outcome-told-back · female M1/M3 retention tracked SEPARATELY · founding-cohort "felt respected not sorted" survey.
