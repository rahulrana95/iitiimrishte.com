# Team Role: Moat, Risks & Metrics (Strategy)

_Raw agent output (condensed)._

Incumbent ran ~12 years, 3× Karan Johar, still ~30k active of ~150k (~20%) at 60/40 — an unmet market, not a saturated one.

## Moat (ranked by durability)
1. **Verified-liquidity flywheel** — dense reachable active pool per market; two-sided + local network effects; 2–4 yrs to copy (must rebuild density per market).
2. **Multi-signal verification network** — per-country credential graph + earned badge trust; 18–36mo. Incumbent verifies a degree, not a person (structural gap).
3. **Trust brand** ("verified≠vouched," privacy-first, fair, no elitism baggage) — 2–3 yrs, scandal-free only.
4. **Proprietary matching & fairness data** (reply/reachability/outcome-labeled) — 2–4 yrs.
5. **Verification/credential partnerships** — 12–24mo, exclusivity lock.
6. **International compliance posture** (DPDP+GDPR+residency) — 9–18mo, moat by attrition.
**Decisive:** durable moat = #1+#2 fused (verified liquidity); rest reinforces. A verification badge with a dead pool is worthless (incumbent's failure).

## Competitive threats & defense
- **iitiimshaadi:** adds KYC, drops price, more KJo/PR → out-execute on liquidity+fairness+speed; attack weakest flank publicly (honest active metrics, reply parity); broaden beyond 3-institute ceiling.
- **Shaadi/EliteMatrimony:** mass-brand-anchored, "premium" reads as upsell → own international verified-professional + privacy/fairness they can't credibly claim.
- **Aisle:** out-verify + international + marriage-intent (they skew India-resident, relationship-not-marriage).
- **The League/Raya:** dating, US-centric, not marriage-intent, culturally thin for diaspora.
- **Dil Mil (sharpest threat):** real 2M+ diaspora liquidity but casual DNA → **win diaspora density in 1–2 hubs FAST** on verification+marriage-intent before they retrofit.
- **New funded entrant:** money can't buy verified local density or scandal-free trust brand fast → first-mover on density is the game.

## North-Star Metric
**Monthly Verified Mutual Conversations (VMC)** — mutual matches between two verified members reaching sustained two-way conversation (≥10 messages, both parties, ≥3 days). Impossible to hit without acquisition + verification + liquidity + fairness + intent all working; leads marriages by 6–18mo.
**Guardrail counter-metric (reported beside NSM, never traded against): Reply-Rate Parity** |male−female reply rate| ≤ target — prevents juicing VMC by over-serving the advantaged side.

## Metric tree (targets)
Acquisition: waitlist→register ≥40%, CAC ≤$60 self-serve/≤$400 concierge · Verification: completion ≥65%, verified-active ratio ≥70%, time-to-verify ≤24h, fraud catch ≥95% · Liquidity: gender ratio ≤55:45 (target 52:48), reachable matches/active/mo ≥8, ≥3,000 active to declare market "live" · Matches: interest→mutual ≥20%, first-reply rate ≥35%, **reply parity ≤10pts (24mo ≤8)**, match→convo ≥25% · Outcomes: relationships tracked/published, outcome NPS ≥40 · Revenue: free→paid 6–10%, ARPU $28–45/$300+concierge, NRR ≥90%, LTV:CAC ≥3:1.

## The one thing + pre-mortem
**The one thing:** guaranteed verified liquidity in the first segment before charging. Launch narrow, hit density (≥3,000 active verified, ≤55:45), prove reply parity, then expand.
- **Failure 1 (most likely): cold-start death** (spread thin) → beachhead first + density gate before charging + reachability guarantee + waitlist scarcity + seed scarcer side first.
- **Failure 2: gender/response imbalance** → reply parity as hard guardrail; acquire/subsidize scarcer side; fairness mechanics; gender-symmetric verification.
- **Failure 3: trust rupture** (breach/fraud/safety) → isolated vault + encryption + fraud models + identity-linked bans + "verified≠vouched" + pre-drafted incident-response + named safety lead pre-launch + moderation SLA.

## Regulatory/reputational + governance
